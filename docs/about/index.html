About Diffusion Doctor

Welcome to Diffusion Doctor, a blog dedicated to showcasing the latest and greatest in AI-generated images. Our focus is on stable diffusion models, which allow for the creation of high-quality images with a wide range of applications.

From art and design to scientific visualization, we explore the possibilities and limitations of this cutting-edge technology. Check in every week for new articles and exciting examples of AI-generated images.
It is not our goal to only show the most shiny results, but also failed attempts and learnings from the process.
